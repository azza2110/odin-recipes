Peanut Butter on Toast

Description

A delicious breakfast when you can't be bothered to make anything else.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Crunchy peanut butter
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Butter one side of both pieces of toast
  2. Apply peanut butter to the same side of both pieces of toast
  3. Sprinkle the toast with the slightest pinch of salt
